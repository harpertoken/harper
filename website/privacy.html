PRIVACY POLICY

Data Collection
Harper processes your commands locally and communicates with AI providers. We do not collect or store your personal data on our servers.
AI Providers
Commands are sent to your chosen AI provider (OpenAI, Sambanova, or Gemini). Review their respective privacy policies for data handling.
Local Storage
Harper stores conversation history and settings locally on your device. This data is never transmitted to our servers.
